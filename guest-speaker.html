IASAS MUN 2014
13-15 November 2014 | Jakarta International School

Guest Speaker: Paul Lemaistre

Paul is a 2003 JIS alumnus and was an avid Model United Nations club member and IASAS delegate during his time here. After JIS he went to Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts where he majored in history and international relations. After Tufts he earned his masters degree in political science from the London School of Economics and Political Science in the United Kingdom.

Paul currently works as an environment and climate change specialist based here in Jakarta. He works with a wide range of partners, including the Indonesian government, donor organisations, private companies and local Indonesian communities on a variety or projects related to the improved management and conservation of Indonesia's forests.

His professional experiences have included researching international trade policy as a staff member for a Member of Parliament in the UK's House of Commons; writing speeches and attending global summits with the Secretary-General of the ASEAN Secretariat; and designing projects to protect Indonesia's forests with the World Bank in Jakarta.
